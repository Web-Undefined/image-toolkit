HEIC is the photo format iPhones use by default, but Windows, older Android phones, and many websites can't open it. This free converter turns HEIC into universally supported JPG right in your browser — your photos are never uploaded to a server.

Frequently asked questions

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?
No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device.
Why won't my HEIC files open on Windows?
HEIC is Apple-preferred. Many Windows and Android apps lack a decoder, so converting to JPG makes photos open everywhere.
Is there a limit on how many files I can convert?
You can convert many at once. Very large images (over 50 MB each) may be skipped to avoid running out of browser memory.
